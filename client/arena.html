Methodology

How the Elo rating system determines the best AI video model.

01

Blind Comparison

Two AI models generate videos or images from the same prompt. Viewers see both videos without knowing which model produced which ensuring pure quality judgment.

02

Elo Rating System

Originally designed for chess, the Elo system calculates relative skill levels. Winners gain points, losers lose points. The magnitude depends on the expected outcome.

03

Expected Score

E_A = 1 / (1 + 10^((R_B - R_A) / 400))

The expected score predicts how likely a model is to win based on current ratings. Upsetting a higher rated model yields more points.

04

Rating Update

R'_A = R_A + K × (S_A - E_A)

After each battle, ratings update using the K-factor (set to 32). The actual result is compared against expectations to produce the new rating.

05

Convergence

Over hundreds of battles, ratings stabilize to reflect true model strength. The more battles a model participates in, the more reliable its rating becomes.

06

Fairness Guarantees

Models are randomly paired. Prompt diversity prevents specialization. Blind voting eliminates brand bias. The system is mathematically self-correcting.

About Eden AI

Building the definitive benchmark for AI video generation.

The Mission

AI video and image generation are evolving at an incredible pace. But one question remains: which AI model is actually the best? Most benchmarks today are unreliable they can be biased, selectively curated, or disconnected from real world quality. Eden Arena solves this by introducing a crowd powered evaluation platform where AI models compete head to head under identical conditions. From cinematic videos to creative images, every model is judged through fair comparisons and real human feedback.

Why Elo?

The Elo rating system has been the standard for competitive ranking since the 1960s. It powers systems like chess, esports, and matchmaking platforms around the world. By adapting Elo to AI video and image generation, we create a ranking system that is: Fair · Dynamic · Self correcting over time Every vote improves the rankings, making the system smarter with each battle.

Open & Transparent

All battle results are public. Ratings are updated continuously. The methodology is designed around transparency and community participation. We believe the best way to evaluate AI generation is through open competition, unbiased comparisons, and the collective judgment of the community.

Every vote matters. Whether you're a researcher, developer, creator, or AI enthusiast, your judgment helps shape the future of AI generation. Join the arena, compare the world's leading AI video and image models, cast your votes, and help determine which models truly stand above the rest.
